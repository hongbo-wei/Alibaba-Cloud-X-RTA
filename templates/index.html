Boxing Qualities

Build a solid foundation, and then focus on the upper layer.

  • Boxing IQ
  • Reflex
  • Speed
  • Endurance
  • Strength

How so?

Boxing is divided into different weight classes. And fighters normally won't have the strength that belongs to much higher weight.

  • Strength matters, but it won't exceed too much for a certian weight.
  • Endurance allows boxers to fight in many rounds, not burn out early.
  • Speed determines explosive power, and help timing.
  • Reflex is the ability to react to momentory situations.
  • With above 4 quliaties, higher boxing IQ always prevails.
